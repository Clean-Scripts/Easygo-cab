The EasyGo Journey

Redefining travel from the bustling streets of Omaxe City 1 to every breathtaking corner of Madhya Pradesh.

Our Story: Driven by Passion

Welcome to EasyGo Cabs. Founded and operated right here in Indore by Nishant Jaiswal, our mission started with a simple observation: navigating our beautiful state shouldn't be a hassle. Operating from our base at Omaxe City 1, Indore, we recognized the need for a transportation service that blends absolute transparency with premium comfort.

When you book with EasyGo, you aren't just getting a cab; you are stepping into a meticulously maintained, crisp white Citroen C3. We chose this vehicle specifically for its superior suspension, spacious interior, and smooth handling—ensuring that whether you are bouncing over city speed breakers or cruising on the super-corridor highway, your ride remains impeccably smooth.

"We believe in upfront, honest pricing. That is why our structure is locked in: A flat base fare of ₹70, and a straightforward ₹15/km after 20km. No hidden surges. Just you, the open road, and a reliable partner."

Privacy Policy

Effective Date: January 1, 2026

Welcome to EasyGo Cabs. We are strictly committed to protecting your personal information. If you have any questions, contact us via WhatsApp.

1. Information We Collect

  • Personal Data: Name and valid 10-digit phone numbers.
  • Location Data: Pickup and drop-off coordinates during the booking process.
  • Security Data: One-Time Passwords (OTPs) generated entirely on the platform for your security.

2. Secure Google Sheets DB

EasyGo utilizes advanced Google Workspace tools. When you submit a booking or review, details are securely transmitted via encrypted fetch API to our private database. We DO NOT sell, trade, or rent your data.

3. Strict OTP Visibility

The Driver (Nishant) DOES NOT receive your OTP via WhatsApp or SMS. The OTP is solely displayed on your active dashboard screen. You must physically share it to start the ride.
