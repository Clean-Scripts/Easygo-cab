The EasyGo Journey

Redefining travel from the bustling streets of Omaxe City 1 to every breathtaking corner of Madhya Pradesh.

Our Story: Driven by Passion

Welcome to EasyGo Cabs. Founded and operated right here in Indore by Nishant Jaiswal, our mission started with a simple observation: navigating our beautiful state shouldn't be a hassle. Operating from our base at Omaxe City 1, Indore, we recognized the need for a transportation service that blends absolute transparency with premium comfort.

When you book with EasyGo, you aren't just getting a cab; you are stepping into a meticulously maintained, crisp white Citroen C3. We chose this vehicle specifically for its superior suspension, spacious interior, and smooth handling—ensuring that whether you are bouncing over city speed breakers or cruising on the super-corridor highway, your ride remains impeccably smooth.
