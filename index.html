The EasyGo Chronicles

Where It All Began

Founded by Nishant Jaiswal in Omaxe City 1, Indore, EasyGo Cabs emerged from a simple frustration: unfair surge pricing and unpredictable service. Nishant decided to put his own White Citroën C3 into service — a car representing elegance and comfort.

Unlike aggregators, EasyGo is a 100% self-owned service. No middlemen, no hidden commissions — just pure, honest mobility. Today, with over 2,500+ happy rides and a 4.98⭐ rating, we are Indore's most trusted choice.
