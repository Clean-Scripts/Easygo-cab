Our Story: Driven by Passion

Welcome to EasyGo Cabs. Founded and operated right here in Indore by Nishant Jaiswal, our mission started with a simple observation: navigating our beautiful state shouldn't be a hassle. Operating from our base near 114 Prime Square, Omaxe City 1, we recognized the need for a transportation service that blends absolute transparency with premium comfort.

When you book with EasyGo, you aren't just getting a cab; you are stepping into a meticulously maintained, crisp white Citroen C3. We chose this vehicle specifically for its superior suspension, spacious interior, and smooth handling—ensuring that whether you are bouncing over city speed breakers or cruising on the super-corridor highway, your ride remains impeccably smooth.

We believe in upfront, honest pricing. That is why our structure is locked in: A flat base fare of ₹70, and a straightforward ₹15/km after 20km. No hidden surges when it rains, no confusing algorithms. Just you, the open road, and a reliable partner to get you to your destination.

Ultimate Tourist Destinations in MP

Madhya Pradesh is the heart of Incredible India. Whether it's a spiritual journey, a heritage trail, or a foodie adventure, our EasyGo cabs are ready to take you there. Check out the top destinations you can visit from Indore.

Ujjain - Mahakaleshwar

Located just 55 km from Indore, Ujjain is a city of spiritual awakening. Home to one of the 12 Jyotirlingas, the Mahakaleshwar Temple draws millions of devotees. With EasyGo, you can book a quiet, comfortable morning ride for the Bhasma Aarti and travel back in absolute peace.

Distance: ~55 KM from Indore

Omkareshwar & Maheshwar

Experience the divine Narmada River. Omkareshwar offers another sacred Jyotirlinga situated on an island shaped like the holy 'Om'. A short drive away is Maheshwar, famous for its magnificent Ahilya Fort and exquisite Maheshwari sarees. A perfect full-day trip in our Citroen C3.

Distance: ~80 to 95 KM from Indore

Mandu (Mandavgad)

The city of joy and romance! Mandu is an ancient fort city renowned for its Afghan architecture. Visit the Jahaz Mahal (Ship Palace), Hindola Mahal, and Rani Rupmati Pavilion. The winding, scenic roads up the Vindhya range are a joy to experience in our premium cabs.

Distance: ~97 KM from Indore

Indore City Tour

Don't want to leave the city? Let us take you on an Indore food and heritage trail. From the majestic Rajwada Palace and Lal Bagh Palace to the street food havens of Chappan Dukan and the late-night Sarafa Bazaar. EasyGo ensures you won't have to worry about finding parking!

Privacy Policy

Effective Date: January 1, 2026

Welcome to EasyGo Cabs. We are committed to protecting your personal information and your right to privacy. If you have any questions or concerns about this privacy notice or our practices with regard to your personal information, please contact us via WhatsApp.

1. Information We Collect

We collect personal information that you voluntarily provide to us when you register on the website, express an interest in obtaining information about us or our products and services, or when you contact us. The personal information that we collect depends on the context of your interactions with us. The personal information we collect may include the following:

  • Personal Identification Data: Name, phone numbers, and email addresses.
  • Location Data: Pickup and drop-off coordinates, saved addresses, and live routing data during the ride.
  • Authentication Data: One-Time Passwords (OTPs) generated for secure logins and ride verification.

2. Database & Google Sheets Integration

In our effort to maintain a highly secure, fast, and robust infrastructure, EasyGo Cabs utilizes advanced Google Workspace tools, including Google Sheets via Google Apps Script, to manage our database. When you submit a booking, your details (Phone, Pickup, Drop, Date, Time, and OTP) are securely transmitted via fetch API to our private, encrypted Google Sheets database. This ensures zero data loss and immediate logging of all transactions.

3. How We Use Your Information

We use the information we collect or receive:

  • To facilitate account creation and logon process.
  • To fulfill and manage your ride bookings seamlessly.
  • To communicate directly via WhatsApp for booking confirmations and driver tracking.
  • To calculate accurate fares based on distance and waiting times.

4. Information Sharing

We DO NOT sell, trade, or rent your personal identification information to others. Your pickup and drop locations, along with your phone number, are only shared with your assigned driver to ensure successful pickup and drop operations.

Terms and Conditions

Please read these terms and conditions carefully before using the EasyGo cab booking platform operated by Nishant Jaiswal.

Section 1: General Booking & Services

By placing a booking through our website, you warrant that you are legally capable of entering into binding contracts. The service provided involves the transportation of passengers from the specified pickup point to the drop-off destination in our designated vehicle (Citroen C3).

Section 2: Pricing & Fare Structure

EasyGo prides itself on a transparent pricing model. The fare structure is rigidly set as follows:

  • Base Fare: ₹70 is applicable for the initiation of the ride and covers distances up to the threshold of 20 kilometers.
  • Per Kilometer Charge: Upon exceeding the initial 20 kilometers, a charge of ₹15 per kilometer will be levied.
  • Toll Taxes & Parking: Any state tolls, municipal taxes, or parking fees incurred during the journey are the sole responsibility of the customer and will be added to the final bill.

Section 3: Waiting Time Policy

Time is valuable for both our drivers and our customers. To ensure operational efficiency:

Waiting Time Charge: ₹1 per 30 seconds.

This charge activates exactly when the driver arrives at the designated pickup location. The timer halts only when the ride officially begins (which is verified by the customer sharing the OTP with the driver). We request customers to be ready at the pickup point upon driver arrival.

Section 4: OTP & Security Verification

For passenger safety, every ride booked via EasyGo generates a unique One-Time Password (OTP). It is mandatory to provide this OTP to the driver before the commencement of the journey. Refusal to provide the OTP will result in ride cancellation.

Section 5: Customer Conduct

Passengers are expected to maintain the cleanliness and decorum of the vehicle. Smoking, consumption of alcohol, or eating messy items inside the cab is strictly prohibited. Any damage or extreme dirtiness caused to the vehicle's interior by the passenger will result in a cleaning fee of up to ₹1000.
